## Why They Need a Better Website / AI Automation

### 1. Bay Utilization = Revenue — Empty Bays Are Wasted Money
4 private bays sitting idle during off-peak hours (weekday mornings, Sunday afternoons) is direct revenue loss. Most simulator clubs don't have automated campaigns to fill low-utilization windows.
**Pitch:** Automated "slow period" SMS blasts to members (e.g., "Bay 2 open this Thursday 10am–12pm — book now for 20% off"). Fill the gaps, no manual outreach needed.

### 2. Membership Churn Is the #1 Killer for Clubs Like This
If a member hasn't booked in 3+ weeks, they're at high churn risk. Without an automated win-back sequence, they quietly cancel.
**Pitch:** Automated engagement triggers — "We haven't seen you in a while, here's a complimentary hour" — keeps members active and renewing.

### 3. Corporate Events & Bachelor Parties Are High-Ticket, Under-Marketed
A 4-hour corporate event booking across 4 bays + food/drinks could be $500–$1,500+ in one visit. These high-value bookings need a dedicated page and automated inquiry follow-up.
**Pitch:** Events landing page + automated response to group inquiry forms (immediate reply, then 24hr follow-up if no conversion).

### 4. BYOB + Social Atmosphere Is a Differentiator — Leverage It
"BYOB golf" with friends is a unique positioning in the Irmo/Columbia market. This angle resonates on social and converts walk-ins.
**Pitch:** Social media retargeting + a "Book a Night Out" campaign page. We handle the creative and the follow-up automation.
